Treaty 2

Map of the Treaty

"Treaty 2 was signed 21 August 1871 between Canada
and the Anishinaabe of southern Manitoba ."
-The Canadian Encyclopedia Article Treaties 1 and 2

"Treaty Two was created to discuss matters of interest between
the First Nations peoples and Her Majesty, Queen Victoria.
Her Majesty wished to have British immigrants settle the
land that the First Nations peoples inhabited, and the treaty
was created to receive their consent."
-Government of Treaty 2 Territory Website

Communities involved

"The treaty involved many smaller tribes of Chippewa Natives:"
-Canadian History Workshop Treaty 2


"The Sousonse people of Swan Creek
and Lake Manitoba,"

"A man by the name of Keweetahquunnayash

"The Masahkeeyash people of Fairford and

"the Francois people of Waterhen River,
